## 1. Factual Accuracy (As of April 2026)

- **OBU Installation Deadlines:** The draft says installation is "continuing into 2026." You should be more specific: LTA has mandated that all Singapore-registered vehicles must have the OBU installed by January 1, 2027.
- **Final Reminder Window:** As of now (April 2026), vehicle owners who haven't switched from the old IU to the OBU are likely in their "final reminder" period. If they don't install it within 3 months of their notice, they lose the free installation and must pay a fee (~$70 for cars).
- **ERP Rates:** Your "Typical weekday charges" are slightly low for 2026. Following the March 23, 2026 adjustments: CTE Southbound can hit S$5.00 during peak (08:35–08:55); KPE/ECP now hits S$6.00 during heaviest peak; AYE City-bound peak slots now cost ~S$4.00 after a S$1 increase in March 2026.
- **The "Empty IU" Fine:** Your FAQ says "typically S$10." To be precise: if you miss a payment you now get a 5-day grace period to pay the base charge via OBU/App/OneMotoring without the $10 administrative fee. Miss that window and the $10 fee is added. Ignore further and the fine jumps to ~S$70.
